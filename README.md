# 🏦 Bank Customer Churn Analysis & Retention Strategy Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This end-to-end data analytics project analyzes customer churn behavior in the banking sector using SQL, Python, Excel, and Power BI. The objective is to identify key factors influencing customer attrition, uncover churn patterns, and provide actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Customer churn directly impacts a bank's profitability and growth. Understanding why customers leave helps financial institutions develop targeted retention strategies, improve customer satisfaction, and reduce revenue loss.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Customers aged 40–60 years showed a higher tendency to churn compared to younger customers.
- Inactive members exhibited significantly higher churn rates than active members.
- Churn rates varied across different geographic regions, highlighting location-based retention challenges.
- Customers with higher account balances were more likely to leave the bank in specific customer segments.
- Product ownership influenced retention, with customers holding multiple products generally showing better loyalty.
- Customer engagement and activity levels had a stronger impact on churn than credit score alone.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Implement targeted retention campaigns for inactive customers.
- Develop personalized offers and loyalty programs for high-value customers.
- Increase customer engagement through proactive communication and support.
- Monitor regions with above-average churn rates and address local retention challenges.
- Encourage customers to adopt additional banking products and services to improve retention.
